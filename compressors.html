Compressors

Osmium unlocks the diagnostic data you need from your variable-speed drives and gives you fine control of their operation

Osmium introduces real-time, two-way communication with your drives, PLCs, motors, pumps and other industrial equipment, giving you a level of visibility and control normally available only using on-site using HMIs.



Diagnostics reporting

See how your compressors are performing in real-time, with alerts to let you know if something is wrong.

24/7 visibility and control

Start and stop compressors remotely, schedule operation to suit your needs and reduce energy consumption.

Performance analysis

Monitor key performance indicators such as run hours, load/unload cycles and energy consumption to optimise efficiency.



Customised user access

Define different user types e.g. engineer, operator, manager, guest and set user-specific and device-specific levels of system access.

HMI on your phone

Remote access to human-machine interface (HMI) data and commands previously available only on-site, avoids unnecessary site visits and accelerates fault diagnosis and fix.

Configured remotely

Osmium is tailored remotely to meet the requirements of your business and to integrate with your legacy equipment. It can be integrated into existing control panels where required.
