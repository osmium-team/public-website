Reduce your energy costs

Reduce energy costs with our three-step process:

From rail stations through to modular buildings and HVAC, we are enabling organsiations to reduce energy costs by following these three steps:

(1) Get on the right tariff: use our tariff studio matching engine to pick the best available tariff based on your actual half-hourly energy use data. If you don't have data we will work with your in-house electricians to add energy monitoring capability at the main incomers to your buildings. First results available at the end of week 1.

(2) Reduce energy use: root out energy waste and identify potential improvements through circuit-level monitoring. It can be surprising what you find: hours‑of‑use issues, out‑of‑hours loads, oversized plant, poor power factors, heaters being turned on before they are needed or left on unnecessarily over the weekend. .

(3) Compliance: If you are a large organisation, get ahead on preparing for mandatory compliance with ESOS Phase 4 legislation and feed data directly into an ESOS audit platform and the UK government’s MESOS system.
