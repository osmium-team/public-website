Modular buildings

Monitor energy use in modular buildings in real time and track location.

Our modular buildings tracking and energy monitoring product, developed with our partners Europa PLC, allows modular buildings providers and their customers to keep track of energy use and cabin location.

The unit comes with a standard socket on a short trailling lead and is fastened directly onto the side of a cabin. Since January 2026 the depth of the enclosure is just 107mm which fits comfortably within the standard haulage safe zone .
